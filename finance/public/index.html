ABOUT US

Finance Club, NIT Silchar, is a student-run organization aimed at fostering and developing financial knowledge and interest among students. With a dedicated team, we strive to promote various finance-related fields, offering opportunities for students to explore through workshops, competitions, projects, and interactive sessions. Our goal is to equip students with practical finance skills and insights to help them excel in the evolving financial world.

INVESTMENT TEAM

The Finance Club at NIT Silchar runs an Investment Team, where over 30 members actively manage real money across three key verticals: Equity Research, Venture Capital, and Quantitative Finance. The team fosters engaging discussions on markets and global economics, while providing students with hands-on experience in financial markets. This practical exposure helps them develop deeper insights into investment strategies and market dynamics.
